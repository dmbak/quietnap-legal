Privacy Policy — QuietNap

Last updated: January 2026

QuietNap respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

QuietNap does not collect or store any personal data on external servers.

All app settings (selected sounds, alarms, nap duration, Pro status) are stored locally on your device.

Health Data

If you enable Apple Health sync, QuietNap may write completed nap sessions to Apple Health as sleep records.

Health data is handled entirely by Apple Health

QuietNap does not read, upload, or share health data

Health data is never transmitted outside your device

You can disable Apple Health sync at any time in the app settings.

Purchases

QuietNap uses Apple’s in-app purchase system.
All payments and subscription management are handled by Apple. QuietNap does not access your payment information.

Third-Party Services

QuietNap does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.
